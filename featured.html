New Artists and Tracks

PinkPantheress released her debut studio album, "Heaven Knows," on November 10, 2023, following her 2021 debut mixtape, "To Hell With It." The album's journey was marked by delays, as PinkPantheress grappled with the pressure of meeting expectations. In interviews in 2022 and 2023, she expressed concerns and a desire to explore diverse musical styles before the album's eventual release.

The album's rollout included the lead single, "Boy's a Liar Pt. 2" featuring Ice Spice, released on February 3, 2023. Despite earlier speculations about an October release, the album's cover art, title, and tracklist were unveiled on October 12, coinciding with the release of "Capable of Love," the third single. With twelve tracks and featuring artists like Central Cee, Rema, and Kelela, "Heaven Knows" emerged as a significant milestone in PinkPantheress' musical evolution, showcasing her versatility and depth as an artist.
